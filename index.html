About Me

Hello there! I am a Filmmaker, Producer, Director, and Director of Photography in Bandung,Indonesia.
Often I see images and have ideas that I would like to share with others. This is how my films come about. I enjoy experimenting so my work covers a wide range of subjects, styles and techniques: documentaries, short film, experimental and more!
I have an great understanding of editing and basic colour correction ranging across short film and documentary . I Am using the Adobe Premier Pro.
